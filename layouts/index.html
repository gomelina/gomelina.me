About our lessons

Sobre nuestras lecciones

Our lessons are online & 1:1 for 60 minutes.

Book a lesson at the time that suits you best, I am available everyday from 07:00 am to 10:00 pm.

I will plan our next lessons based on your progress, needs, and interests. We will decide topics together, practices, and methods.

We'll have short talks in Spanish to see your progress every lesson. We'll practice your reading, writing, listening, understanding, and grammar.

I'll prepare a presentation for each lesson in both Spanish & English, with examples and exercises. I'll send everything I prepare for you so you can practice between lessons.

Communication between us is important for your Spanish learning process. I want you to tell me how are you feeling with lessons, topics, and how I can help you the most.
